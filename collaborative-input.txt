SIGNAL THEORY FRAMEWORK (MASTER REFERENCE):

Four Signal States:
1. Interest - Reciprocal effort, consistent forward movement, she initiates, follows through on plans
2. Uncertainty - Inconsistent patterns, warm but distant after, vague about plans, evaluating
3. Comfort - Easy connection, emotional openness, but no romantic direction or escalation
4. Disengagement - Moving away, slower responses, plans falling through, fading energy

Core Framework Rules:
- Signals Over Stories: Trust behavior not explanations. When words and behavior conflict, behavior wins.
- Timing Over Intent: When you act matters more than why. Kind intentions early create pressure.
- Calibration Over Performance: Match energy to situation. Don't try to impress or force connection.
- The Master Question: "Am I aligned with the signal or trying to change it?" If trying to change it, stop.

Key Insight: You don't fix signals. You respond to them. Goal is accuracy not performance.
