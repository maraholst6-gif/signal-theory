## Restructure Recommendation

**ALL 30 questions need the same format:**

**Standard Answer Template:**
- **A) Interest** - She's showing reciprocal effort, consistent forward movement, initiating contact or plans
- **B) Uncertainty** - She's evaluating but hasn't decided, inconsistent patterns, warm but distant
- **C) Comfort** - Easy connection exists but no romantic direction or escalation happening
- **D) Disengagement** - She's moving away, fading energy, plans falling through, slower responses

**Each scenario should clearly map to ONE of these states based on observable behaviors.**
