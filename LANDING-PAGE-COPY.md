## SOLUTION SECTION

### Section Headline
**The Signal Theory Framework: Stop Telling Stories. Start Seeing Reality.**

### Body Copy
Signal Theory is built on one core principle:

**Believe what people show you, not the stories you tell yourself.**

Most dating advice teaches you tricks, tactics, or how to "get" someone to like you. Signal Theory teaches you something better: **how to read reality and respond to it.**

Because when you can see what's actually happening—not what you hope is happening—you stop wasting time on dead ends and start investing energy where it actually matters.

### How It Works (3-Step Process)

**1. Learn the Framework**  
Understand the four signal states: Positive, Neutral, Negative, and Ambiguous. Train yourself to recognize the difference between what she's *saying* and what she's *showing*.

**2. Practice Real Scenarios**  
Work through realistic dating situations and learn how to respond—not with manipulation or games, but with clarity and confidence.

**3. Apply It in Real Life**  
Use the AI analyzer to get instant feedback on your own situations. Stop second-guessing yourself. Start seeing clearly.
